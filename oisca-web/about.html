OUR STORY

Not a mall.
A real world for children.

Every other option in Bangkok is indoor and artificial. We're the only one that isn't. Founded by OISCA International — Japan's oldest and largest environmental NPO — our school was built on a single conviction: children learn best when they interact with the real world.

For 16 years, families from Japan, Europe, Australia, Thailand, and across the Middle East have chosen OISCA for one reason above all others: there is nowhere else in Bangkok where their children can play in a flowing stream, run across real grass, and come home dirty every single day.
