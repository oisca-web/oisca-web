OUR STORY

Not a mall.
A real world for children.

Every other option in Bangkok is indoor and artificial. We're the only one that isn't. Founded by OISCA International — Japan's largest environmental NGO — our school was built on one belief: children learn best when the classroom is the real world.

For 16 years, families from Japan, Europe, Australia, Thailand, and across the Middle East have chosen OISCA because of what we have that no one else in Bangkok does: a real stream, real grass, real soil, and real trees — right in the heart of Sukhumvit.
