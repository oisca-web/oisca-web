CAMPUS ZONES

Every corner is
built for learning.

Six distinct outdoor zones, each designed for a different kind of play and discovery. Open every school day.

💧
Real Stream
A living waterway where children measure flow, find bugs, and learn that water is alive. Bangkok's only kindergarten stream.
UNIQUE
🌿
Grass Field
1,200 ㎡ of real turf — rare in Bangkok. Space to run, roll, and feel the ground beneath your feet.
1,200㎡
⛰
Climbing Mounds
Natural earth mounds with a jackfruit tree and a mango tree — balance, spatial awareness, and a little wild adventure.
OUTDOOR
🏖
Covered Sandbox
A shaded sandbox big enough for collaborative digging, building, and sensory exploration whatever the weather.
ALL WEATHER
🎨
Classrooms
Light-filled indoor spaces that open directly onto the outdoor zones — inside and outside flow naturally together.
