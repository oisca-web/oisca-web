CAMPUS ZONES

Every corner is
built for learning.

Six distinct outdoor zones, each designed for a different kind of play and discovery. Open every school day.

💧
Real Flowing Stream
Bangkok's only kindergarten with a real stone-lined stream. Children dam rivers, float objects, and discover physics through water play every single day.
UNIQUE IN BANGKOK
🌿
Natural Grass Field
Full 1,200㎡ of real turf with UV-blocking shade nets. Run, roll, do cartwheels — regardless of the Bangkok heat.
1,200㎡
🏖️
Covered Sandbox
Large covered sand area open rain or shine. Fully stocked with tricycles, diggers, and building tools at all times.
RAIN OR SHINE
⛰️
Climbing Mounds
Two large grass mounds for climbing, sliding, and conquering. Physical challenge that builds real confidence and gross motor skills.
2 HILLS
🌳
Fruit Trees & Garden
Jackfruit, plumeria, and native Thai plants throughout the campus. Children watch seasons change, plant seeds, and harvest real food.
LIVING · GROWING

Indoor Classrooms
Air-conditioned classrooms with natural light and nature views. British EYFS curriculum delivered by qualified international teachers.
