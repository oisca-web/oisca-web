Common Questions

FAQ

Can I pay monthly instead of per term?↓
Yes. Monthly payment is available. The per-term price is slightly discounted compared to paying monthly.
What documents do I need for enrollment?↓
Passport copy and birth certificate. Collected in person on first visit. Medical records (vaccination history) also requested.
Is the enrollment fee refundable?↓
No. The enrollment fee is non-refundable once paid. Tuition fees paid per term are non-refundable after the term starts.
Can my child try before enrolling?↓
Yes. We offer a free trial day (Mon–Fri 8am–4pm). Contact us to book.
日本語での対応は可能ですか？↓
はい、日本語でのご対応が可能です。日本語の管理スタッフが在籍しています。
