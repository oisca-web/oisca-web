About OISCA

Where Bangkok meets
the natural world.

Established in 2008 by OISCA International — Japan's largest NPO — our campus brings real nature into the city. Children learn through water, soil, trees, and play.

💧
Real Flowing Stream
Bangkok's only kindergarten with a real stone-lined stream. Children dam rivers, float objects, and discover physics every day.
Active · Unique in Bangkok
🌿
Natural Grass Field
Full 1,200㎡ of real turf with shade nets — rare in Bangkok. Run, roll, and play regardless of the heat.
Active · 1,200㎡
🏖️
Covered Sandbox
Large covered sand area open rain or shine. Tricycles, diggers, and building tools always available.
Active · Rain or shine
⛰️
Climbing Mounds
Two large mounds for climbing, sliding, and conquering. Physical challenge that builds real confidence.
Active · 2 hills
🌳
Fruit Trees & Garden
Jackfruit, plumeria, and native Thai plants. Children watch seasons change and harvest real food.
Growing · Living archive
🚌
School Bus Network
Door-to-door service across Sukhumvit Soi 24–69. Morning drop-off and afternoon pick-up available.

Why OISCA

Not a mall.
A real world for children.

Every other option in Bangkok is indoor and artificial. We're the only one that isn't.

🌍

International community

Families from Japan, Europe, Australia, Thailand, and the Middle East. Your child grows up in a genuinely global environment.

🇬🇧

British curriculum

Structured learning that prepares children for international schools worldwide — delivered outdoors whenever possible.

🧠

Nature-first learning

Stream physics, gardening, outdoor exploration. Children learn deeper and retain more when the classroom is the world.
